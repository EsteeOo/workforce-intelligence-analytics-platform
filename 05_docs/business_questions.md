# Business Questions

## Workforce and Retention
- Where is attrition highest across countries, sites, and departments?
- Where is early attrition concentrated within the workforce?
- Which workforce segments demonstrate elevated risk patterns?

## Hiring Effectiveness
- Is hiring volume aligned with workforce demand?
- Which stages of the hiring funnel show the greatest drop-off?
- Which roles or locations are consistently difficult to fill?

## Capability and Readiness
- Where are training completion levels lowest?
- Which areas show high levels of overdue or incomplete training?
- How does capability readiness align with workforce risk?

## Engagement and Workforce Experience
- Which areas show low engagement, wellbeing, or manager support?
- How does employee experience correlate with retention and workforce pressure?
- Where are consistent negative patterns observed in workforce sentiment?

## DEI and Representation
- What is the composition of the workforce across key demographic dimensions?
- Are there differences in hiring outcomes or attrition across groups?
- Which areas show imbalances in representation?

## Executive Focus
- Which areas require prioritised intervention?
- Where do combined signals across hiring, retention, capability, and workforce pressure indicate elevated risk?
- What actions should be prioritised based on current workforce conditions?
