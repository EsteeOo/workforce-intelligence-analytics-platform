# Curated Layer Plan

## Objective
Provide reporting-ready analytical marts that support executive and operational workforce reporting.

## Curated Marts
- mart_workforce
- mart_hiring
- mart_learning
- mart_engagement
- mart_operations
- mart_site_summary

## Design Principles
- Aggregate at business-relevant levels
- Preserve interpretability for reporting
- Separate domain-specific marts from cross-domain summary outputs
- Support downstream semantic modelling in Power BI
