# Stakeholder Map

| Stakeholder | Primary Focus | Reporting Needs |
|---|---|---|
| Chief Human Resources Officer | Workforce strategy and risk | Attrition, workforce health, intervention priorities |
| VP People / People Director | Cross-functional workforce visibility | Capability, engagement, organisational risk |
| Talent Acquisition Lead | Hiring delivery and efficiency | Funnel performance, open roles, time to hire |
| Learning and Development Lead | Workforce capability | Training completion, overdue learning, readiness gaps |
| HR Business Partners | Functional workforce oversight | Department and site-level workforce patterns |
| Site Leaders | Local workforce performance | Overtime, absence, staffing pressure, attrition |
| Executive Leadership Team | Consolidated reporting view | KPI summaries, trends, high-risk areas |
| BI / Data Team | Data governance and control | Data quality, lineage, metric consistency |
