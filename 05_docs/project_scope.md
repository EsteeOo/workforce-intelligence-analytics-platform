# Workforce Intelligence Analytics Platform

## Platform Summary
The Workforce Intelligence Analytics Platform provides a unified analytics environment for workforce performance across hiring, retention, capability, engagement, and workforce operations.

The platform consolidates multiple workforce data domains into a governed reporting layer, enabling consistent KPI reporting, cross-functional visibility, and structured decision-making.

## Purpose
The platform supports enterprise workforce monitoring by delivering a reliable and structured view of workforce dynamics, allowing leadership to identify risks, assess performance, and prioritise interventions.

## Core Capabilities
- Integration of multiple workforce data domains into a single reporting model
- Structured transformation layers across raw, staging, and curated datasets
- Semantic modelling to support consistent KPI definitions
- Executive and operational reporting with drilldown capability
- Governance artefacts including metric definitions, lineage, and data quality controls
- Defined refresh and orchestration logic supporting scalable operation

## Outputs
- Multi-domain workforce dataset
- Structured SQL transformation layers
- Power BI semantic model and reporting layer
- Data dictionary and metric definitions
- KPI traceability and lineage mapping
- Architecture and data flow diagrams
- Automation and refresh design
