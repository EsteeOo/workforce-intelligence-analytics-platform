# Metric Dictionary

## Workforce

**Attrition Rate**  
attrition_count / headcount

**Early Attrition Rate**  
early_attrition_count / headcount

## Hiring

**Hire Rate**  
hires / applications

**Offer Conversion Rate**  
offers / interviews

## Engagement

**Average Engagement Score**  
Mean of engagement scores

**Intent to Leave Rate**  
intent_to_leave_count / survey_count

## Learning

**Completion Rate**  
completed_modules / total_assigned

## Operations

**Overtime Rate**  
avg_overtime_hours / avg_scheduled_hours

**Absence Rate**  
avg_absence_hours / avg_scheduled_hours

**Pressure Rate**  
Derived composite metric combining overtime, absence, and workload indicators

## Additional Metrics

**Median Salary**  
Median value of employee salary distribution

**Average Manager Support**  
Mean score of manager support ratings

**Intent to Leave Count**  
Total employees indicating likelihood to leave

**High Overtime Count**  
Instances where overtime exceeds threshold

**Pressure Record Count**  
Total records contributing to pressure analysis

**Survey Count**  
Total engagement survey responses

**Offer Conversion Rate**  
offers / interviews

**Interview Conversion Rate**  
interviews / applications
